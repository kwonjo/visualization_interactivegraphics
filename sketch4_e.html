What is Artificial Intelligence (AI) in films?

  • What is Artificial Intelligence /ärdəˈfiSHəl inˈteləjəns/? 
1: a branch of computer science dealing with the simulation of intelligent behavior in computers<br>
2: the capability of a machine to imitate intelligent human behavior
 
  • What is the project about?
My MA thesis is on AI and I wanted to explore more on AI in various areas. I enjoy watching movies and movies have an impact on how people think of a specific issue. I have read Neosentience The Benevolence Engine which mentions films related to AI. The movies introduced in the book are A.I.(2001) and I, Robot(2004). The recent movie of AI that has been revolutionary is Her(2013). I used the three scripts from these films to analyze the texts to observe how AI was being portrayed by films. 
 
  • How was the piece created?
 

    1. The Data set 

    Movie scripts: A.I., I, Robot, and Her

    2. Topic Modeling with Mallet 

    According to Blei, topic models are algorithms for discovering the main themes that pervade a large and otherwise unstructured collection of documents. Topic models can organize the collection according to the discovered themes. (from Probablistic Topic Models)

    bin/mallet train-topics  --input ai.mallet --num-topics 10 --output-state ai2.gz --output-topic-keys ai_keys2.txt --output-doc-topics ai_composition2.txt --optimize-interval 15

    Topic modeling enabled the movie scripts to be organized into 10 topics with the optimal interval of 15. 

    3. Color Selection

    Every year Pantone introduces the color of the year and the color of the year 2018 is ultra violet(18-3838).I have always had the interest in Pantone's selections and this year's color was attractive to me. The "Floral Fantasies" selection seemed interesting and I have wanted to incorporate it in some of my work. I have used ultra violet, dark violet, peach, and lettuce green for this piece.
